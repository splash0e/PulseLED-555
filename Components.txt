# Components Used


i) NE555 Timer IC


ii) Resistors: R1 = 56kΩ, R2 = 56kΩ, R = 8.8 or 10kΩ


iii) Capacitor: C = 10µF, 25V


iv) LED (1.5V–2V) any colour of choice (RGB).


v) Power Supply: +5V DC (Make sure voltage won't cross above the rating)


vi) Breadboard and Connecting Wires (make sure connection isn't lose and tighten properly)
